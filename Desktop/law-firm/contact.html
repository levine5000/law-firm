Common Questions

How will you keep my information confidential?

We take confidentiality very seriously. All communications and documents related to your case are kept secure, and we adhere to strict ethical guidelines to protect your privacy.

How do you handle settlements versus going to trial?

We evaluate each case to determine the best strategy. We often seek settlements to avoid the costs and uncertainties of a trial, but if a fair settlement isn't offered, we are prepared to go to court.

Can you explain the litigation process in detail?

Certainly! The litigation process includes several stages: pleadings, discovery, motions, trial, and potentially appeals. We’ll guide you through each stage and explain what to expect.

What is the timeline for my case?

The timeline can vary depending on the complexity of the case and the court's schedule. Generally, it can take several months to over a year. We’ll keep you updated on key milestones as we progress.
